INGREDIENTS

    Group 1

  • 1000g Strong flour (chilled)
  • 400-450g Cold water
  • 15g Salt
  • 30g Sugar
  • 60g Butter
  • 10g Bread improver
  • 20g Milk powder
  • 20g Fresh yeast
  • 2 Large eggs (100g)

    • Group 2

  • 500g Roll-in butter (chilled)

METHOD

  1. Combine group one, mix on slow speed for one minute, and develop on second speed
    for 4-5 minutes. Develop the dough to around 80%.

  2. Cover with plastic, and rest in chiller for 10 minutes.

  3. Flatten group 2 butter into a sheet approx. 1cm thick, and keep chilled.

  4. Process dough as per french method by rolling the dough out into a square enough that the sheet
    of butter can be placed like a diamond and wrapped with the corners of the square piece of dough

  5. Give the dough one half turn, cover and rest in fridge for 10 minutes (Do this 3 times).
    This will give 135 layers.

  6. Roll out dough to around 3.5mm thick, and cut out into triangle shapes, curl into croissant
    shape and place on a lightly greased tray

  7. Egg wash and prove at 30°c with a relative humidity of 70% for around 30 minutes.

  8. Bake at 230°c for 10-15 minutes.
